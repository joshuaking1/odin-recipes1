Raspberry Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing

This raspberry balsamic dressing tastes fantastic on a salad with fruit (apples are my favorite) or even as a bread dip! I was tired of trying bottle after bottle of dressing and not finding anything close to my favorite restaurant-inspired salad until I made this recipe.

Ingredients

  1. ¼ cup vegetable oil
  2. 2 tablespoons raspberry balsamic vinegar
  3. 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  4. 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  5. 1 clove garlic, minced
  6. ½ teaspoon honey Dijon mustard
